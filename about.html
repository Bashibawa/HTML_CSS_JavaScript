About Barter Trades

Barter Trades was founded in 2021 by a team of Seven(7). Our aim is to avoid the wastage of Human Resources & Perishable goods due to low circulation of money especially among active entreprenuers.

One could live in a country with less or no financial transaction. We believe money may not be every thing. Your skill and produce cannot be worth by only currency rather by values you can offer, so that even when inflation or deflation of currencies take place your value is still retained.


Our team is divided into 7 Sections listed below;

  1. Administration & Management
  2. Human Resource & Personeel
  3. Customer care service
  4. Web design & development
  5. Graphic design & Advertisement
  6. ICP & Cyber security
  7. Account & Data Management
