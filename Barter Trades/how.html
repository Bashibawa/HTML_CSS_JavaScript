How to Trade by Barter Online

Start Trading Today...

Five Easy Steps

  1. Create an account Here or Login to your account.
  2. Create a Post by uploading Four (4) Photos of your product or service you render with proper description and Price per unit product/ service.
  3. Search for the goods or services you'll need in the Catalouge section.
  4. Bargain per unit with your customer in the Chat Room.
  5. Leave a Feedback & Rating on the customer's profile. This would be public to other potential customers.

Note: We only have Insurance Policy for our Premium Traders.

However, it is very safe to transact with Basic Traders with positive Ratings & Feedbacks. For more Information read our Terms and Conditions.
